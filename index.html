About

Born and raised in Long Beach, CA. I discovered my love of creative writing when I was 8, and I enrolled into the Orange County School of the Arts (OCSA) for High School, one of the best decisions of my life. I grew as a writer, made many new friends, and found opportunties that I couldn't have ever found anywhere else.



Throughout high school, I've been able to see my writing take numerous forms. I have seen several short plays of mine come to life on the screen.






Several of my creations can be found online, such as a play performed in 2014: The Rites of Adulthood. A short film I produced is also available on YouTube, called 95.
